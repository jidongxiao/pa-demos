Why use fake typing in your presentations?

Our fake typing feature allows you to upload prewritten code or text and have it “typed out” automatically during your live presentation. This helps you:
  • Maintain a natural flow when demonstrating code.
  • Highlight important concepts step by step for your audience.
  • Save time and reduce cognitive load during live demos.
  • Ensure consistency when teaching or presenting exercises.
And when you're ready for full-featured collaboration, our production code editor on PresentationAssistants.com allows one presenter to control the session while hundreds of participants follow along, run code, and interact in real time. Participants are view-only unless you explicitly grant edit permissions.
